About

Tanner Waldo

HR works best when the compliance side and the people side are pointed at the same goal. A policy that managers can’t execute is just documentation. A people decision that ignores compliance is a liability waiting to land.

My background is in frontline retail operations. I spent years managing teams before moving into HR, which means I know what policy feels like for the people on the floor, on the phones, where the work meets the customer. That shapes everything I do in this field.

This site covers compliance and human rights, performance management, leading teams, and the books worth reading on this work. The writing is aimed at HR practitioners and the managers they support.
