    People & Culture support for frontline teams.

    I worked with store and district leaders on performance, conduct, leaves, and the day‑to‑day questions that affected staffing, coverage, and morale.

    As part of a three person team, I supported 8 District Directors and about 130 Store Managers across Canada on employee relations and investigations, performance conversations, leaves and accommodations, and making sure policy fit how stores actually ran.

    On visits, I walked the floor, talked with whoever was on shift, and asked how work felt in practice; I used what I heard, along with engagement results and active cases, to help leaders make decisions that were fair to people and defensible for the company.
